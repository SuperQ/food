# Belgian Liege Waffles

## Ingredients
* 470g Pastry flour (750g)
* 170g Warm milk (270g)
* 40g fresh yeast (70g)
* 2 eggs
* 1 egg yolks
* Vanilla sugar
* Salt

* 250g butter room temp (400g)
* 300g sugar (500g)

## Prepare dough
* Mix flour, milk, yeast, eggs, vanilla, salt.
* Rest 30 minutes.
* Work butter and pearl sugar into dough.
* Portion into 100-125g.
* Rest 15 minutes.
